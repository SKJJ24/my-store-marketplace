## 1. PURPOSE

This Agreement is made for the purpose of discussing a potential business partnership related to an e-commerce marketplace platform.

---

## 2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:

- Business plans and strategies
- Technical information and source code
- Customer data and user information
- Financial information and projections
- Marketing strategies and plans
- Database structures and designs
- Any other information marked as "Confidential"

---

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

The Receiving Party agrees to:

a) Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential
b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without written consent
c) Use Confidential Information only for the purpose stated above
d) Protect the information with the same degree of care used for their own confidential information
e) Not copy, reproduce, or reverse engineer any materials or systems

---

## 4. EXCLUSIONS

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

a) Was already known to the Receiving Party before disclosure
b) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party
c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party
d) Is rightfully received from a third party without breach of this Agreement

---

## 5. TERM

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **[2/3/5] years** from the date of signing, or until terminated by written notice by either party.

The obligations regarding Confidential Information shall survive termination for an additional **[2/3/5] years**.

---

## 6. RETURN OF MATERIALS

Upon request or termination of discussions, the Receiving Party agrees to:

- Return or destroy all Confidential Information
- Provide written confirmation of destruction if requested
- Delete all electronic copies and backups

---

## 7. NO PARTNERSHIP

This Agreement does not:

- Create a partnership or joint venture
- Obligate either party to enter into any business relationship
- Grant any license or rights to intellectual property

---

## 8. REMEDIES

Both parties acknowledge that:

- Breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm
- Monetary damages may be insufficient remedy
- The Disclosing Party may seek injunctive relief in addition to other remedies

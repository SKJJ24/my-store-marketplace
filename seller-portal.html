Seller Terms & Conditions

1. Seller Agreement

By registering as a seller on MyStoreZW, you agree to these terms and conditions.

2. Seller Responsibilities

Sellers must provide accurate product descriptions, maintain adequate stock, and fulfill orders promptly.

3. Commission Structure

MyStoreZW charges a commission on each sale to maintain the platform. Commission rates vary by category.

4. Product Listings

All products must comply with Zimbabwe laws. Prohibited items include counterfeit goods, weapons, and illegal substances.

5. Payment & Payouts

Sellers receive payment within 7-14 business days after order delivery and customer confirmation.

6. Account Termination

MyStoreZW reserves the right to suspend or terminate seller accounts that violate these terms.
